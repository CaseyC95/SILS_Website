About INLS490

This course revolves around the very idea of artificial intelligence, or AI. It covers such topics as: intelligence, understanding, and intentionality in the context of AI and various types thereof; speculative philosophy and ethics of AI given relaxed definitions of these concepts; the philosophical implications of merging with AI; and the creative representation of AI in literature and film.
